# Divine Iris Healing - Admin Panel Guide

> Your complete guide to managing every part of your website. Written so anyone can follow along!

---

## How to Access the Admin Panel

1. Go to your website and add `/admin` at the end (e.g., `yoursite.com/admin`)
2. Enter your **Username** and **Password**
3. You're in! You'll see a sidebar on the left with all the tabs

## Tab-by-Tab Guide

---

### 1. Hero Banner

**What is it?** The very first thing visitors see when they land on your homepage - the big beautiful banner at the top with your name, tagline, and background image.

**What can you change?**
- **Background Image** — Upload or paste a URL for the hero background
- **Title Text** — The main heading (e.g., "Divine Iris Healing")
- **Subtitle Text** — The tagline below the title (e.g., "ETERNAL HAPPINESS")
- **CTA Button** — The call-to-action button text and where it links to
- **Overlay** — Darken/lighten the background so text is readable

**When to use:** When you want to change the first impression of your website — new photo, new tagline, seasonal update.

---

### 2. Homepage

**What is it?** The MASTER CONTROL for your entire homepage. This is where you manage every section that appears on the homepage.

**What can you change?**

**For each section (About, Upcoming, Sponsor, Programs, Sessions, Stats, Testimonials, Newsletter):**
- **Title** — The section heading visitors see (e.g., "Upcoming Programs")
- **Subtitle** — Text below the heading (e.g., "CHOOSE YOUR HAPPINESS")
- **Font Controls** — For BOTH title and subtitle:
  - **Font Family** — Choose the typeface (Lato, Cinzel, Playfair Display, etc.)
  - **Font Size** — How big the text is (e.g., 18px, 24px, 36px)
  - **Font Color** — Click the color swatch to pick any color
  - **Bold** — Toggle bold on/off
  - **Italic** — Toggle italic on/off
- **Visibility Toggle** — Show/hide any section from the homepage
- **Reorder** — Use the up/down arrows to move sections higher or lower on the page

**"Edit Section Content" button** (for About, Sponsor, Newsletter):
- Click this to expand and edit the actual CONTENT of that section (images, body text, descriptions) — all without leaving this tab!

**When to use:**
- Rearranging your homepage layout? Use the arrows
- Changing a section title or its font/color? Edit it here
- Want to hide a section temporarily? Toggle visibility off
- Need to update About bio, Sponsor description, or Newsletter text? Click "Edit Section Content"

**Important:** This is the ONE place for all section titles, subtitles, and their fonts. Don't look elsewhere!

### 3. Page Headers

**What is it?** Controls the banner/hero image at the TOP of every sub-page (not the homepage). Each page like /about, /programs, /services, /contact has its own header banner.

**What can you change?**
- **Background Image** — The banner photo for each sub-page
- **Overlay Color & Opacity** — Darken the image so text shows clearly
- **Height** — How tall the banner area is

**When to use:** When you want to change the decorative banner photo on pages like About, Programs, Services, Contact.

**Not the same as Homepage tab!** Homepage controls the main page sections. Page Headers controls the small banner at the top of OTHER pages.

### 4. Programs

**What is it?** Where you create and manage your healing programs (e.g., "Quad Layer Healing", "AWRP").

**What can you change?**
- **Add New Program** — Create a brand new program
- **Program Title** — The name of the program
- **Description** — What the program is about
- **Image** — The program's cover photo
- **Duration & Dates** — When it runs, session count
- **Pricing** — Set prices in INR, USD, and AED for each tier
- **Enrollment Status** — "Open", "Coming Soon", or "Closed"
- **Visibility** — Show/hide from the website
- **Tiers** — Create different levels (e.g., Standard, Premium, VIP)

**When to use:**
- Launching a new program? Click "+ Add Program"
- Program full? Change enrollment status to "Closed"
- Need to update pricing? Edit the program and change prices
- Want to hide a program temporarily? Toggle visibility off

**Note:** The program CARDS you see on the homepage get their content from here. The section TITLE ("Flagship Programs") and its font are controlled from the Homepage tab.

## Tips

1. **Always click "Save Changes"** after making edits in Hero Banner, Homepage, Page Headers, Header & Footer, or Global Styles tabs
2. **The Homepage tab is your best friend** — most homepage changes happen here
3. **Programs and Sessions tabs** are for managing content (what exists). The Homepage tab controls how their sections LOOK (titles, fonts)
4. **Preview your changes** — Click "View Site" in the top-right corner after saving
