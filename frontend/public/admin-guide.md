# Divine Iris Healing - Admin Panel Guide

> Your complete guide to managing every part of your website. Written so anyone can follow along!

---

## How to Access the Admin Panel

1. Go to your website and add `/admin` at the end (e.g., `yoursite.com/admin`)
2. Enter your **Username** and **Password**
3. You're in! You'll see a sidebar on the left with all the tabs

---

## Admin Panel Navigation

The admin sidebar is organized into 6 collapsible groups:

### Group 1: WEBSITE
Hero Banner, Homepage, Page Headers, Stats, Header & Footer, Global Styles, Testimonials, Text Quotes

### Group 2: PROGRAMS & OFFERS
Programs, Pricing Hub, Upcoming Hub, Sessions, Promotions, Discounts & Loyalty, Special/VIP Offers, INR Pricing for NRI, Exchange Rates

### Group 3: TRANSACTIONS
Enrollments, Indian Payment, India Proofs, API Keys, Receipt Template, Fraud Detection

### Group 4: INBOX
Inbox (contact form submissions)

### Group 5: CLIENTS
Client Garden, Subscribers, Annual Subscribers, Scheduler

### Group 6: DASHBOARD
Dashboard Config, Sanctuary Design

Click any group header to expand/collapse. The `<` button in the top bar collapses the entire sidebar to icon-only mode.

## Tab-by-Tab Guide

---

### 1. Hero Banner

**What is it?** The very first thing visitors see when they land on your homepage - the big beautiful banner at the top with your name, tagline, and background image.

**What can you change?**
- **Background Image** — Upload or paste a URL for the hero background
- **Title Text** — The main heading (e.g., "Divine Iris Healing")
- **Subtitle Text** — The tagline below the title (e.g., "ETERNAL HAPPINESS")
- **CTA Button** — The call-to-action button text and where it links to
- **Overlay** — Darken/lighten the background so text is readable

**When to use:** When you want to change the first impression of your website — new photo, new tagline, seasonal update.

---

### 2. Homepage

**What is it?** The MASTER CONTROL for your entire homepage. This is where you manage every section that appears on the homepage.

**What can you change?**

**For each section (About, Upcoming, Sponsor, Programs, Sessions, Stats, Testimonials, Newsletter):**
- **Title** — The section heading visitors see (e.g., "Upcoming Programs")
- **Subtitle** — Text below the heading (e.g., "CHOOSE YOUR HAPPINESS")
- **Font Controls** — For BOTH title and subtitle:
  - **Font Family** — Choose the typeface (Lato, Cinzel, Playfair Display, etc.)
  - **Font Size** — How big the text is (e.g., 18px, 24px, 36px)
  - **Font Color** — Click the color swatch to pick any color
  - **Bold** — Toggle bold on/off
  - **Italic** — Toggle italic on/off
- **Visibility Toggle** — Show/hide any section from the homepage
- **Reorder** — Use the up/down arrows to move sections higher or lower on the page

**"Edit Section Content" button** (for About, Sponsor, Newsletter):
- Click this to expand and edit the actual CONTENT of that section (images, body text, descriptions) — all without leaving this tab!

**When to use:**
- Rearranging your homepage layout? Use the arrows
- Changing a section title or its font/color? Edit it here
- Want to hide a section temporarily? Toggle visibility off
- Need to update About bio, Sponsor description, or Newsletter text? Click "Edit Section Content"

**Important:** This is the ONE place for all section titles, subtitles, and their fonts. Don't look elsewhere!

---

### 3. Page Headers

**What is it?** Controls the banner/hero image at the TOP of every sub-page (not the homepage). Each page like /about, /programs, /services, /contact has its own header banner.

**What can you change?**
- **Background Image** — The banner photo for each sub-page
- **Overlay Color & Opacity** — Darken the image so text shows clearly
- **Height** — How tall the banner area is

**When to use:** When you want to change the decorative banner photo on pages like About, Programs, Services, Contact.

**Not the same as Homepage tab!** Homepage controls the main page sections. Page Headers controls the small banner at the top of OTHER pages.

---

### 4. Programs

**What is it?** Where you create and manage your healing programs (e.g., "Quad Layer Healing", "AWRP").

**What can you change?**
- **Add New Program** — Create a brand new program
- **Program Title** — The name of the program
- **Description** — What the program is about
- **Image** — The program's cover photo
- **Duration & Dates** — When it runs, session count
- **Pricing** — Set prices in INR, USD, and AED for each tier
- **Enrollment Status** — "Open", "Coming Soon", or "Closed"
- **Visibility** — Show/hide from the website
- **Tiers** — Create different levels (e.g., Standard, Premium, VIP)

**When to use:**
- Launching a new program? Click "+ Add Program"
- Program full? Change enrollment status to "Closed"
- Need to update pricing? Edit the program and change prices
- Want to hide a program temporarily? Toggle visibility off

**Note:** The program CARDS you see on the homepage get their content from here. The section TITLE ("Flagship Programs") and its font are controlled from the Homepage tab.

---

### 5. Pricing Hub

**What is it?** A centralized dashboard to control pricing visibility and attendance modes for ALL programs and sessions at once.

**What can you change?**
- **Show/Hide Pricing** — Toggle whether prices are visible on the website for each program or session
- **Attendance Modes** — Enable/disable Online, Offline, In-Person for each program or session

**When to use:** Quick way to show/hide prices across your site, or switch attendance modes without editing each program individually.

---

### 6. Upcoming Hub

**What is it?** Controls which programs appear in the "Upcoming Programs" section on the homepage.

**What can you change?**
- Which programs are marked as "upcoming"
- Their display order

**When to use:** When you want to feature specific programs in the Upcoming section.

---

### 7. Sessions

**What is it?** Where you create and manage your personal healing sessions (e.g., "Ancestral Healing", "Chakra Healing", "Akashic Record Reading").

**What can you change?**
- **Add New Session** — Create a new personal session type
- **Session Name** — The name visitors see
- **Duration** — How long the session lasts
- **Description** — What the session involves
- **Pricing** — Prices in INR, USD, AED
- **Session Type** — Online, Remote, In-Person
- **Visibility** — Show/hide from the website
- **Available Dates & Times** — When the session can be booked
- **Element Visibility & Order** — Control which elements (name, type, duration, price, calendar, etc.) appear in the session list and detail view on the homepage

**When to use:**
- Adding a new type of session? Click "+ Add Session"
- Changing session pricing? Edit the session
- Want to control what info shows in the session cards? Use the Element Visibility toggles at the top

- **Add Testimonial** — Add a new video testimonial

- **Client Name** — Who gave the testimonial

- **Visibility & Order** — Show/hide and reorder

### 9. Text Quotes

**What can you change?**
- **Add Quote** — Add a new text testimonial
- **Quote Text** — What the client said

- **Visibility & Order** — Show/hide and reorder

**When to use:** When you want to add or update the scrolling quote testimonials.

---

### 10. Stats

**What is it?** The statistics bar on the homepage showing numbers like "500+ Lives Healed", "100+ Programs", etc.

**What can you change?**
- **Add Stat** — Add a new stat counter
- **Number** — The count (e.g., "500+")
- **Label** — What the number means (e.g., "Lives Healed")

**When to use:** When your numbers grow and you want to update them, or when you want to add a new achievement counter.

---

### 11. Header & Footer

**What is it?** Controls what appears in the website's top navigation bar and bottom footer on EVERY page.

**What can you change?**
- **Header:**
  - Logo image
  - Navigation menu items (labels, links, order)
  - Menu visibility
- **Footer:**
  - Footer menu links
  - Contact email
  - Social media links
  - Copyright text
  - Footer description text

**When to use:** When you want to add a new page link to the menu, change your contact email, or update social media links.

**What can you see?**

- **Payment status** — Paid, pending, etc.
- **Date** — When they enrolled

**When to use:** To check who enrolled, verify payments, or export data for your records.

- **Add Promotion** — Create a new promotional announcement

### 16. Discounts & Loyalty

**What can you change?**
- **Exchange rates** between INR, USD, and AED

### 18. Subscribers

**What can you see?**

### 19. Global Styles

**What is it?** Site-wide design settings that affect the overall look of your entire website.

**What can you change?**
- **Global Fonts** — Set the default heading and body fonts for the whole site
- **Colors** — Primary, secondary, accent colors
- **Spacing & Layout** — Global spacing preferences

**When to use:** When you want to change the overall look and feel of the entire website at once (e.g., switch all headings to a new font).

### 20. Payments

**What is it?** Payment gateway settings and configuration.

**What can you change?**
- **Payment methods** — Enable/disable payment options
- **Bank account details** — For manual/bank transfer payments
- **Payment instructions** — What clients see during checkout

**When to use:** When you need to update bank details, add a new payment method, or change checkout instructions.

---

### 21. India Proofs

**What is it?** Manage payment proof uploads for Indian clients who pay via bank transfer/UPI.

**What can you see?**
- **Uploaded proofs** — Screenshots/photos of payment confirmations
- **Client details** — Who uploaded it
- **Verification status** — Approved/pending

**When to use:** When Indian clients pay via bank transfer and upload proof — you verify and approve here.

---

### 22. Fraud Detection

**What is it?** Security system that flags suspicious enrollments based on location, payment method, and other signals.

**What can you see?**
- **Fraud alerts** — Flagged transactions with severity level
- **Alert details** — Why it was flagged (VPN, country mismatch, etc.)
- **Status** — New, reviewed, resolved
- **Email Blocklist** — Block specific email addresses from enrolling
- **Alert email** — Set where fraud notifications are sent

**When to use:** To review flagged transactions and block bad actors. Check this regularly.

---

### 23. Receipt Template

**What is it?** Customize the receipt/invoice that clients receive after payment.

**What can you change?**
- **Logo** — Your business logo on receipts
- **Header/Footer text** — Custom text on the receipt
- **Fields** — Which information appears on the receipt

**When to use:** When you want to customize how your payment receipts look.

---

### 24. API Keys

**What is it?** Technical settings for third-party service integrations.

**What can you change?**
- **API keys** for services like Stripe, email, etc.

**When to use:** Only when setting up or changing payment/email integrations. **Be careful** — changing these can break things!

## New Features Guide

---

### Admin Security

**Change Password:** Click "Change Password" in the top admin bar → enter new password → Save. Use a strong password — the old one is no longer shown on the login page.

---

### FOMO Rotating Subtitles (Homepage → Upcoming section)

The subtitle under "Upcoming Programs" now rotates through multiple messages with fade animation.

**How to edit:** Homepage tab → expand Upcoming section → "Rotating FOMO Subtitles" panel → Add/edit messages like "SEATS ARE FILLING FAST", "YOUR TRANSFORMATION AWAITS"

---

### Enrollment Urgency Testimonials

Rotating one-liner quotes on the enrollment page that create urgency.

**How to edit:** Upcoming Hub → scroll to "Enrollment Urgency Testimonials" → Add testimonials with name (e.g., "Joining was my best decision" — Anita R.)

---

### Combo Discounts (Discounts & Loyalty)

Tiered discounts when multiple programs are in cart.

**How to set up:**
1. Go to Discounts & Loyalty → Combo Discount → Toggle ON
2. Add Rule → Set: If 2+ programs → 10% off, code: COMBO2
3. Add another: If 3+ programs → 15% off, code: COMBO3
4. Select which programs qualify (click program names)
5. Save

---

### Cross-Sell Discounts (Discounts & Loyalty)

"Buy Program A → Get discount on Program B" offers.

**How to set up:**
1. Toggle ON Cross-Sell
2. Add Rule → Select "Buy" program (e.g., AWRP) + optional tier (1 Month)
3. Add target program(s) → Set discount % or fixed amount
4. Each rule gets a tracking code
5. Shows on homepage as golden info strip

---

### Special/VIP Offers (Programs & Offers)

Target specific people by email/phone with exclusive discounts.

**How to set up:**
1. Add Offer → Name it (e.g., "Family Discount")
2. Set discount % or fixed amount
3. Add people: Type email/phone one by one OR upload CSV
4. Select which programs this applies to
5. Enable/disable each offer independently

---

### INR Pricing for NRI Students (Programs & Offers)

3 ways to give INR pricing to Indian students living abroad:

1. **Email Whitelist** — Add student emails → they auto-get INR when they verify email
2. **Invite Links** — Generate token → share link → student gets INR pricing
3. **INR Promo Code** — Create in Promotions tab → forces INR pricing

---

### India Payment Gateway (Indian Payment)

Configure your India payment gateway:

1. Toggle Enable → Select type (Exly Link / Razorpay / PayU)
2. For Exly: Just paste the payment URL
3. For Razorpay: Paste API Key + Secret
4. Save

---

### Bank Accounts (India Proofs)

Add multiple Divine Iris bank accounts. Users choose which account they paid to.

**How to add:** India Proofs → scroll to "Bank Accounts" → Add Account → Fill label, account name, number, IFSC, bank, branch → Save

---

### Manual Payment Proofs (India Proofs)

When users submit manual payment proof:

1. New proofs auto-appear (refreshes every 15 seconds)
2. Click **View** to see full attachment + all details
3. Click **Edit** to modify details before approving
4. Click **Approve** → receipt is sent + enrollment is completed
5. Click **Reject** to decline

---

### Enrollment Tracking (Enrollments)

All enrollments from every source in one place:

- **Payment Mode**: Stripe (blue) / Bank Transfer (green) / Manual Proof (amber) / Exly (purple) / Free
- **Filter** by status or payment mode
- **Search** by name, email, phone, invoice number
- **Click any row** to expand full details
- **Download Excel** exports everything — nothing is missed

Invoice numbers format: YYYY-MM-001 (e.g., 2026-03-001)

---

### GST Invoice (Receipt Template)

For India payments, receipts include proper GST breakdown:

1. Receipt Template → "GST Invoice (India Only)" → Toggle ON
2. Fill: Company Name, GSTIN, PAN, Address, GST Rate (18%), Type (IGST)
3. Preview shows exact invoice format
4. Only appears on INR receipts — international receipts unchanged

### Currency & VPN

- **India**: Only genuine Indian IP (no VPN) gets INR pricing
- **Everyone else**: VPN ignored — they see local currency
- **Stripe charges in local currency** (EUR, CAD, AUD, etc.) — settles to your AED account
- **Override**: Use INR whitelist/tokens for NRI students

---

### Auto-Close Programs

Programs automatically close when their deadline passes. Status changes to "Registration Closed" and Enroll/Add to Cart buttons are disabled.

---

### Planning Calendar (Upcoming Hub)

Visual calendar at the top of Upcoming Hub:
- Shows all program dates as colored blocks
- Click a date button (Start/End/Deadline) then click a calendar day to set that date
- Collapsible — click header to toggle
